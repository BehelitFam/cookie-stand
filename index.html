About Us

Pat's Salmon Cookies is a family owned business dedicated to combatting our fiercest enemy, the mighty Salmon. Our ancestors have been locked in mortal combat with this deadly and cunning foe for centuries. One day, our great-great-great-grandfather AntiPiscevius, tired of the usual savory preparations, rolled up his fallen nemesis Salmonus Primus with some dough and some chives, he baked it into the very first Salmon Cookie - topped with a dash of sweet cream cheese frosting! Today our family continues this martial and culinary tradition in locations all across the Seattle area. Combining techniques passed down from generation to generation with attention to ecological best practices and some modern innovations, Salmon Cookies are taking the NorthWest by storm for some unholy reason! With my wife Patty, and my kids Bobby Junior and Sweet Sally May, we intend to dominate the novelty confectionary market and rule over our competitors with an iron fist for a thousand years!
